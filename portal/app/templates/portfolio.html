Forging raw factory telemetry into decisions.

FerroFlux is an end-to-end analytics platform for Egyptian steel producers — a Kafka + Spark medallion pipeline, an Airflow-orchestrated batch tier, and an n8n automation layer, all wrapped in a row-isolated multi-tenant portal. Streaming market prices in, KPIs and anomaly alerts out.

The Medallion Pipeline

Raw multi-tenant CSV and live Kafka streams enter at Bronze, are cleaned and enriched with steel-specific metrics at Silver, then aggregated into dashboard-ready KPIs at Gold. Every layer writes a uniform company_id / factory_id / … partition hierarchy.

01 — ingest
BRONZE

Lands market, production, orders, shipments & raw-material feeds as immutable Parquet plus a raw_data Postgres mirror.

Two Doors, One Platform

FerroFlux runs as a public engineering portfolio and a secure SaaS workspace from the same FastAPI service.

Mode A — Public

Portfolio & Live Demo

  • Open dashboard. Market price, throughput & supply-chain latency charts — no signup wall.
  • Architecture story. The medallion flow and container topology, documented in the open.
  • Synthetic stream. Driven by /api/public/metrics so anyone can explore.
Open Live Demo
Mode B — Secure

Multi-Tenant SaaS

  • Row-level isolation. Every query is scoped to your company_id from a signed token.
  • Self-service onboarding. Register factory units with idempotent ON CONFLICT upserts.
  • Data entry. Upload validated Excel feeds that trigger your own Airflow ETL run.
